Our Story

Micorp Trading LLC is a premier general trading company based in Dubai, UAE. With a strategic focus on Electronics, IT Products, and Automobiles, we have evolved into a versatile trading powerhouse serving businesses and consumers globally.


Since our inception, our unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has established us as a reliable source for top-tier products. We bridge the gap between global manufacturers and regional markets, ensuring quality and innovation in every transaction.

Our Mission

To be the preferred general trading partner by offering top-quality products while consistently exceeding customer expectations through reliable service, integrity, and innovative solutions.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in trading by providing innovative, high-quality products, ensuring sustainable growth, and contributing positively to the industries we serve.
