Our Story

Micorp Trading LLC is a premier general trading company based in Dubai, UAE. With a focus on Electronics, IT Products, Automobiles, and Food Stuff, we serve as a trusted partner for businesses and consumers seeking reliable, cutting-edge technology and automotive solutions.


Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has established us as a reliable source for top-tier products that meet the highest industry standards.


Our Mission

To be the preferred general trading partner by offering a broad range of top-quality electronics, IT products, automobiles, and food stuff, while consistently exceeding customer expectations through reliable service, integrity, and innovative solutions.


Our Vision

To become a global leader in trading by providing innovative, high-quality products, ensuring sustainable growth, and contributing positively to the industries we serve.
